Transcribo is **100% offline** and uses **Local LLMs** to understand contextâ€”it can fix grammar, remove filler words ("um," "uh"), or completely rewrite your speech into different formats automatically.
</details>

Yes! Transcribo works **globally**. Whether you're writing in Word, chatting in Discord, coding in VS Code, or posting on Redditâ€”Transcribo types directly where your cursor is.
</details>
